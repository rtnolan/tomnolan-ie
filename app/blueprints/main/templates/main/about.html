About Me

Hi there, my name is Tom, and I live here in the sunny city of Dublin, Ireland. I'm a software engineer and like most of the software engineers I know my hobbies all centre around tech. I'm an avid gamer and also have an interest in music.

The blog you're reading was created using Flask following the great tutorials from @Miguel Grinberg. I've always wanted to give this sort of thing a go and when looking for a new language to learn, I stumbled across Flask.

The topics I'll mostly be blogging about fall into two categories: projects I'm working on or things I find interesting. I currently intend to keep it mostly tech related but who knows.

Thanks for stopping by,
